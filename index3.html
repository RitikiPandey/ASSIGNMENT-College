Swami Vivekananda

|| "Take up one idea. Make that one idea your life; dream of it; think of it; live on that idea. Let the brain, the body, muscles, nerves, every part of your body be full of that idea, and just leave every other idea alone. This is the way to success, and this is the way great spiritual giants are produced.”

'Do Meditation'


Meditation has various advantages but its main advantage is that it clears your mind & make it calm. In this calm mind, you have never problems to find what you really need along with the PURPOSE of YOUR LIFE. In my opinion this is the toughest part of life. Once you get this, further journey is very easy. You have to just stuck on this need or purpose at any cost.
